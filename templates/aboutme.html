Machine Learning:
So how did I get into Machine Learning? It all started when I was playing chess and I was curious about how the most prominent chess engine, Stockfish, worked. I did a quick google search and realized that it mainly used a coding method known as Machine Learning. I immediately became interested in this, learning about engines like AlphaZero and AlphaGo, whihc have revolutionized game theory and the way we play those games. The more I learnt about Machine Learning, the more I wanted to go into the field! Although I do not have any experience working with a real company, I have a passion for building and innovating, and I hope that will come through with my projects.
